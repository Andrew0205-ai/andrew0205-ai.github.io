Privacy Policy

Welcome to Andrew's comment Board. We value your privacy. The following describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information:

1. Collecting Information

We will collect your basic information such as name and email address when you log in with Google(coming soon) in the message board and record the content of your message.

2. Usage Information

The information collected is only used to display messages, manage the message board and improve website functionality, and will not be sold or shared with third parties.

3. Data Protection

We use Firebase database to store comments and take reasonable security measures to protect the data from unauthorized access.

4. Modification and Deletion

You can delete your message at any time, or contact us to request deletion of your information.

Thank you for using Andrew's comment Board!
